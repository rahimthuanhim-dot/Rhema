Welcome

Welcome to Rhema Public Academy

Dear Parents / Guardians

Rhema Public Academy stands for "Excel in Education" for the present era of knowledge explosion. RPA symbolizes a solid foundation on which a successful nation can be built. It is not merely a training ground for academic subjects, but a place for real values — faith, hope, love, compassion, inner discipline, selflessness, intellectual curiosity, and a sense of fairness. Our campus prepares students for life and for further study within the international community.

RPA is situated in Khumji Bazar, Noney district, Manipur, 60 km from Imphal. The campus spans 3 acres, surrounded by the scenic beauty of flowers and trees.

Message from the President

Welcome to Rhema Public Academy. Your children will find a peaceful, brotherly atmosphere here to equip themselves for a bright future. Rhema's trained teachers are passionately committed to helping their students fly higher — and to raising better children who become better citizens.
